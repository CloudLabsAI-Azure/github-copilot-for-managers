## Post-Workshop Action Items

### Week 1: Foundation
- [ ] Review your organization's current Copilot settings
- [ ] Identify 3 potential Copilot champions in your teams
- [ ] Set up a recurring meeting for Copilot adoption review

### Month 1: Measurement
- [ ] Generate your first usage report
- [ ] Create baseline metrics for your teams
- [ ] Share initial findings with leadership

### Quarter 1: Optimization
- [ ] Identify teams needing additional training
- [ ] Document and share successful adoption patterns
- [ ] Present ROI analysis to stakeholders

### Ongoing
- [ ] Monthly usage report review
- [ ] Quarterly ROI updates
- [ ] Annual Copilot program assessment
